PROFILE

Performing academic work in such as conducting lectures and research work in the area of finance, banking and capital markets for  over 12 years. My current area of research involves work in the areas of financial liberalization and asset pricing and have published various articles from my research work. I also have an interest in the area of digital finance and fintech and developed and taught the course at the Faculty of Business and Economics, University of Malaya. I am also actively involved as a researcher in numerous research projects which is funded by local research grants. Prior to working as an academic, I have also had experience working in the areas of banking, business advisory, engineering, manufacturing, real estate and with the central bank.
